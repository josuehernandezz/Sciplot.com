Frequently asked questions

How do I make plots?

To make your own plots, navigate to one of the available plot-types such as, Absorbance and Photoluminesence. Select and upload your data, then fill in the 'label' section with the name you would like to see in the legend of your plot. Make a comma separated list of labels if there are multiple files.

What type of data can I use for plotting?

Currently, our platform supports the following file formats: .txt, .csv, .ssdat, and .xy. We continuously update our platform to extract relevant data from uploaded files. While files can contain non-numerical information, our algorithm skips lines with non-numerical data. For the smoothest experience, we recommend uploading files with two distinct columns using common delimiters like space, comma, or semicolon.
